About SpacePark

SpacePark is the place to go beyond this Earth and explore the great void beyond! Founded in 2018, SpacePark was built to inspire future generations with incredible space attractions, artefacts and informational displays. There's something for everyone in the family from rides to our five-star restaurant where you can enjoy our award-winning Space Burger and Fries. The park is spread over 92 stunning acres of enjoyment, so book your tickets today and become part of the SpacePark Family!

Location

SpacePark is located on a custom-built site right off of the 101 freeway about half an hour from San Francisco Airport. From SFO, travel South on 101 for 26 miles. Look out for the SpacePark signage and giant helium rocket baloon (you can't miss it!). At exit 123 (SpacePark Road), leave the freeway and cross the bridge to main parking. We have space for 3,200 cars.
