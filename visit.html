Transportation

SpacePark is well connected for whatever travel method you choose. Arriving by air? SFO is just 26 miles North of us. By car, we are a short drive south from the airport or about 12 miles North of San Jose, right off the 101 freeway. If you're using transit, SpacePark is on the 42 and 99 bus routes which depart every hour from the San Francisco Embarcadero next to the Ferry Building at the SF Transit Center.

Dining

Astronauts needs to eat well for the demands of space travel! SpacePark has two world-class restaurants, UFO and Yuri's. UFO caters to families of all sizes and includes a self-serve salad bar and fountain drinks area. Yuri's celebrates the best of Russian cuisine, including our world-famous Space Burger. All drinks (not incuding alcohol) are completely free at Yuri's!

Accommodation

With three hotels within a 4-mile radius of SpacePark, you're on your way to a relaxing stay. Try LaunchPad Hotel, out premier partner with free hourly bus to and from SpacePark itself. Every LaunchPad room includes space ice-cream snack bars for the kids and a voice-activated astonomical telescope: Ask it to show you Mars! The nearby Hilton and DoubleTree Hotels are also recommended by SpacePark.

Disabilities

SpacePark prides itself on inclusivity and welcomes all differently-able visitors. All of our rides include accessible options and restraint systems and our staff are fully trained to safely cater and help with any issues which may occur. SpacePark includes dedicated accessible parking at the entrace to the park itself which may also be reserved in advance if needed. SpacePark is accessible to all.
